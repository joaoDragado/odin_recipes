Orange Lukumi

Description

A melt in your mouth confection, the traditional Orange Turkish Delight (Orange Lokum). This gelatin-free and naturally coloured recipe is prepared with very few classic ingredients.

Ingredients for sugar syrup

  • Sugar - 4 cups
  • Water - 1 1/2 cup
  • Lemon
  • Orange Zest - 2 tbsp

Ingredients for lukum

  • Cornstarch - 1 cup
  • Orange juice - 1 cup
  • Water - 2 cup
  • Vinegar - 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Add everything to a pan and boil it till it becomes thick.
  2. Mix all together and cook for 45 minutes.
  3. Pour it to a greased bowl and set it for 6 hours.
